# Teaser Script for "Anara, the Cosmic Weaver"

This draft is written for a short teaser video or live promo clip of about 60 to 90 seconds. It is intended for libraries, schools, cultural centers, festivals, and family audiences.

## Suggested Flow

1. Open with atmosphere
2. Introduce Anara
3. Show what children experience
4. End with an invitation to book

## Sample Script

**Visual:** darkness, a soft glow, a close detail from Anara's illustration  
**Voiceover:**  
"In the darkness of the universe, there is a spider who listens to light."

**Visual:** slow reveal of Anara, hands preparing instruments or storytelling objects  
**Voiceover:**  
"Her name is Anara. She travels across invisible threads, weaving stories about stars, matter, sound, and connection."

**Visual:** fragments of live performance, music, storytelling gesture, children listening  
**Voiceover:**  
"Anara, the Cosmic Weaver is an immersive science storytelling experience for children. Through narrative, live music, images, and interaction, astrophysics becomes something children can feel, imagine, and explore together."

**Visual:** audience participation, reactions, objects or illustrations being revealed  
**Voiceover:**  
"Each session invites young audiences into a poetic universe where questions matter, wonder is shared, and the cosmos becomes close."

**Visual:** final still image or title card  
**Voiceover:**  
"Created for libraries, schools, cultural centers, and festivals, this live format brings art, science, and storytelling into the same luminous space."

**On-screen text:**  
`Anara, the Cosmic Weaver`  
`Book a session or propose a collaboration`  
`sofiag.gallego@gmail.com`

## Alternate Closing Line

"A story to hear, to feel, and to weave together."

## Tone Notes

- Keep the pacing calm, poetic, and spacious.
- Let music and silence breathe between lines.
- Favor close shots, dark backgrounds, and soft light.
- Avoid a commercial tone; the teaser should feel immersive and credible.
