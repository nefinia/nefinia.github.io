02 : Science

I am a Ph.D. in Astrophysics with a strong interest in cosmology and extragalactic astronomy from both theoretical and observational perspectives. In my research I work with data analysis, image & signal processing, and numerical simulations, applying self-developed software in several programming languages as well as specific astronomical software. I did my Ph.D. at ETH Zurich and currently I am a postdoctoral scholar at Caltech.
