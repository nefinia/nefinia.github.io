About

An astrophysicist who works across research, performance, and venture experimentation.

My work moves between rigorous scientific inquiry and public-facing creative formats. I am most interested in places where disciplines overlap and new ways of seeing become possible.

Astrophysics Public communication Music and performance Educational design

Scientist

Research-driven

Postdoctoral astrophysics work focused on galaxy evolution, gas dynamics, protoclusters, and AGN feedback in the early universe.

Artist

Stage-trained

Long-running artistic practice through singing, violin, theater, and live performance across different contexts and countries.

Builder

Format-oriented

I build concepts and interfaces that connect astronomy, education, communication, and culture in more usable ways.

How I Work

Crossing boundaries on purpose

I am drawn to projects that need both depth and translation: strong technical foundations, clear public language, and a format that can actually travel beyond a narrow specialist audience.

  • Research collaboration and scientific communication.
  • Performances and cultural formats linked to science or public events.
  • Microsites, educational experiments, and early-stage venture design.

Current Context

Based in Paris, working internationally

My trajectory has moved through Chile, Switzerland, the United States, and France. That mix shapes both the scientific questions I pursue and the way I design public-facing projects.

  • Languages: English, Español, Français.
  • Open to research, media, cultural, and venture collaborations.
  • Available for talks, curated events, and project conversations.
