NeuroStell
Adaptive learning for institutions

Learning systems should adapt to cognition, not the other way around.

NeuroStell is a science-backed adaptive learning platform that renders the same content differently based on attention, working memory, sensory load, and pacing differences. This proof of concept shows how cognitive profiling, adaptive rendering, and multisensory delivery can work together without diagnosing anyone.

Why it matters

Institutions see drop-offs when learners cannot match their attention rhythm to the delivery format. Adaptive rendering targets this mismatch without demanding extra time from educators.

  • Skimmer: fast orientation and low load.
  • Stepper: guided flow for working memory support.
  • Deep: detailed explanation for mastery.

How the platform works

The PoC maps to the production workflow: profile learners, build interpretable neuro-constellations, adapt content, and deliver through any LMS.

Step 1 Profile

Questionnaires and behavioral tasks infer cognitive preferences.

Step 2 Map

Neuro-Constellations express attention, memory, and sensory load.

Step 3 Adapt

Rule-based logic plus LLMs render the optimal format and pacing.

Step 4 Learn

Deliver through web or LMS with SCORM/xAPI compatibility.

Validation and safeguards

NeuroStell is designed as an educational support tool. It does not diagnose. Validation focuses on learning gain, retention, and cognitive load proxies.

Research loop

Controlled pilots with institutions, followed by peer-reviewed studies.

Privacy by design

Minimal data, de-identified signals, optional exports, GDPR-ready.
