Mini Games

A small collection of experimental puzzle games. Simple rules, different ways of thinking.

Queen Survival

Move like a chess queen and stay alive. Every move changes what remains possible.

Play

Tint Tactics

A strategy puzzle about color, contrast, and local interactions. What looks similar may not behave the same.

Play

XOR Stamps

Recreate a silhouette by stamping shapes. Overlaps cancel. Order doesn’t save you.

Play

CMY XOR Stamps

A colored variation of XOR Stamps using CMY bit-flips. Overlaps cancel per channel; mixing emerges from parity.

Play

Proximity

A proximity-based puzzle: local choices ripple outward. The rule is simple; the edge cases are not.

Play

Hex Wave Puzzle

Match a target field on a circular hex board by placing a limited number of emitters. Rings add up by hex-distance.

Play

Lumen Logic

Place emitters on a walled grid. Light decays by distance and adds up. Match every target value exactly.

Play

Maxwell Demon

A thermodynamics thought experiment turned into a timing game. Open brief “windows” in a wall to separate two particle colors.

Play

PolyPaint

Two players paint the board with polyominoes from a shared pool. Overwrite opponents, aim for maximum coverage.

Play

PolyClear

A duel variant inspired by 9×9 clearing puzzles. Clear rows, columns, and 3×3 blocks for points.

Play

Second Take

Replay difficult moments and explore how situations might unfold differently. A rehearsal game about power, agency, and pause.

Play

Plot Armor

Draw absurd movie logic ingredients and survive impossible threats.
