Cosmoventures SASU

Make complex science rigorous, usable and engaging.

Scientific consulting, R&D support, scientific advising and original educational formats for companies, startups, media and cultural organisations.

Cosmoventures is a scientific innovation and communication studio helping organisations transform complex science into rigorous analysis, usable strategy, credible content and engaging experiences.

Founded by Sofia Gallego, astrophysicist with a research path through ETH Zurich, Caltech and Université Paris Cité.

Cosmoventures envoie une proposition écrite avec livrables, calendrier et prix.

4. Livraison et suivi

Documents finaux, recommandations, présentation, atelier ou session de conseil.
