Outreach

This page gathers the public outreach side of my work: science storytelling, education, cultural astronomy, workshops, and formats that make complex scientific concepts accessible to diverse audiences.

Media

Podcasts, interviews, videos, and long-form conversations where complex ideas become shared dialogue.

Semfiloquia

I am organizing Semfiloquia, the official podcast of SEMF, the Spanish Society for Multidisciplinary Studies. SEMF brings together researchers and thinkers across scientific, philosophical, and cultural fields. The Semfiloquia format is close to traditional colloquia, gathering experts with diverse backgrounds to discuss transversal topics through moderated transdisciplinary dialogue, audience questions, and recorded conversations.

Transdisciplinary Conversations

Transdisciplinary Conversations is my series of dialogues bringing together science, philosophy, art, and society. The goal is to explore deep questions from multiple perspectives, highlighting creativity across disciplines and making space for long-form conversation.

Cosmosofia

Following the same interest in science, art, and communication, in 2021 I created the YouTube channel Cosmosofia. This was an early audiovisual space for sharing scientific and interdisciplinary ideas with a broader audience.

Related Media Work

This media work connects with my broader work in research, outreach, and public engagement: hosting, moderation, audiovisual storytelling, and community-building around scientific ideas. This includes founding Transdisciplinary Conversations at Caltech, co-organizing Astronomy on Tap, and creating Physics on Tap talks at ETH Zurich.

Outreach Projects

Anara, the Cosmic Weaver

A live science storytelling experience for children, where narrative, music, images, and participation open a universe of filaments, wonder, and cosmic listening.

Enter Anara

Cosmoneo

An astronomy-inspired board game where players explore cosmic evolution, galaxies, and hidden connections in the universe. It is currently in beta testing, with plans for expansion.

Read more about Cosmoneo

Bake Your Own Universe

A science outreach activity where participants “cook” the cosmos using edible ingredients to model cosmic processes, linking gastronomy with astrophysics in a playful way.

CosmoTourism

Astro-tourism experiences that connect travelers with observatories, dark-sky reserves, and cultural heritage sites. It grew into a finalist pitch concept for blending astronomy with sustainable travel.
