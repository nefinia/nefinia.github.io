04 : Entrepreneurship

During my time at ETH Zurich in Switzerland, I joined the Innosuisse Startup Campus course “Business Concept,” where I developed the skills, knowledge, and contacts needed to embark on a new entrepreneurial journey. In 2020, I founded "Urania" in my home country, Chile, introducing the innovative concept of "Cosmotourism." This initiative combines astronomical tourism with sustainable accommodation and the promotion of local heritage within the same experience. The first cosmotourism center was launched in the Elqui Valley, Chile.

Building on the success of this initial concept, I am now expanding the Cosmotourism idea into a broader venture aimed at making the wonders of the universe more accessible and engaging. This new phase of the project focuses on integrating educational content with adventure, providing curated experiences designed to ignite curiosity and deepen understanding across various disciplines. While still under development, this venture promises to combine stargazing with practical workshops and inspiring lectures from experts in fields ranging from physics to archaeology.
For more information and updates on this evolving project, please visit cosmotourism.world.

Additionally, I am currently participating in the "Start U Up" program launched by La Cité Internationale Universitaire de Paris. This program offers a comprehensive introduction and guidance on entrepreneurship, featuring workshops, one-on-one mentorship sessions, and hackathons. It's designed specifically for international students and researchers looking to navigate the entrepreneurial landscape in France. I am also excited to be a contestant in the 2024 edition of the International Pitch Night competition organized by the Cité, where I will showcase the potential of my Cosmotourism venture.

In 2021, I also started "Casa Rota", a retail business based on the principles of circular economy and sustainability.
