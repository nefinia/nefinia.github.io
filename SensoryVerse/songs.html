Archetype Songs

These tracks are part of an experimental collection inspired by the SensoryVerse Archetypes. Each archetype represents a way of perceiving, focusing, and creating—and each one is paired with a piece of music designed to complement it.

The titles are two-part: the first part is the archetype name, which comes from symbolic traits found in the archetype profiles, the second part is the song name, a composition inspired by the style and energy of that archetype. For example, The Ethereal Observer – Focus Groove links the archetype with a groove-based track that mirrors its flow.

You can freely explore and listen to any track. If you’d like to know your own archetype (and the music style proposed for it), you can take the quiz below.
