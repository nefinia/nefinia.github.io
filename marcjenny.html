When I first saw Mark and Jenny in the frisbee group I didn’t know they were dating since a week ago, so when I saw them sitting together (I probably had some beers already) I thought they really look very cute as a couple and I started mocking them by saying ouuuu ouuuu. Later they told me they where already dating, and it just made a lot of sense, and that ouu ouu became our little joke, I repeated it every single time I saw them together. So, I wrote a little song about that moment and what their love inspires to us. Love is easy when you're best friends. I hope you enjoy this.
