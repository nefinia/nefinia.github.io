Trajectory

From stage work to science communication

I have performed in musicals, operas, theatrical productions, symphonic settings, and live events. Between 2009 and 2015, performance work also supported my academic path. Later, this developed into channels, conversation formats, and creative projects that make complex ideas more human and memorable.

  • Studies in lyrical singing at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile.
  • Musical theater initiative through ZIMT in Zurich.
  • Cosmosofia and related science-and-culture communication projects.
  • Performance offerings for weddings, receptions, and curated events in Paris.
